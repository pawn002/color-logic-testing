Treatment Tables

  • 75+ is for body and block text
  • 60 is for non-body and non-block text that you want people to read.
  • 45 is for Headlines and art (smallest details >= 4px in smallest dimesnion) that you want people to read.
  • 30 is for any other text not listed previously like disabled element text. Mostly Solid art too like various graph elements (smallest details >= 10px).
  • 15 is absolute minimum that needs to be discernable i.e not invisible. Smallest details >= 15px.
